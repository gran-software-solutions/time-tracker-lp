Time tracking for programmers

Track your time like a software developer. Use the power of ctrl+k to accomplish all time-tracking related tasks

Convenient List and The Timeline View

A traditional list-view allows for a quick overview of the period you are interested in. Additionally it allows you to easily continue any existing task, edit or delete it, or move to another day using a calendar.

The new Timeline View allows you to safely modify a timesheet without overlapping hours. Using only your mouse you can easily modify and move entries around. The Overlap-Guard feature makes sure your day will be fully filled without errors.

Smart Autocomplete

Fuzzy search scoped to a single client allows you to quickly find recurring tasks.

Parallel Tracking

Use your time more efficiently with an instant overview of all running trackers. You can quickly browse, discard or finish tasks without even changing your current context.

Quick Stats

Your favourite statistics are always available. Just open up the drawer by clicking, or pressing the hotkey if you don’t like using mouse.

Quick Actions

Never touch your keyboard again. Using smart shortcuts and intuitive quick actions modal you can accomplish anything without a single click. Manage your trackers, change views, export timesheets, even sign out.

Hotkeys

Blend in hotkeys with your alt key and navigate the app without moving your mouse. Imagine alt-tabbing from your IDE to your browser, then pressing a combo of hotkeys to quickly set up your tracker for a new task.

Powerful Timesheet Editing

Before you export your timesheet for approval, it’s always good to check it up again and see if you missed something. There are various ways to retrospectively edit your timesheet.

You can use the Timeline View and simply drag blocks of time on it, or go to the list view and change your entries with a precision of a minute. You can always add entries manually for any day using the calendar.

Email reminders

Avoid unnecessarily spending time on correcting silly errors. Turn on Email notifications to be reminded if a tracker runs for too long.
